Privacy Policy

Best Evento
Last updated: March 22, 2026

Best Evento ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Best Evento mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in accordance with Google Play policies (2026) and applicable data protection laws.

1. Age Requirement

This App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that data from a user under 18 has been collected, we will delete it immediately upon discovery or request.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Name – provided voluntarily when booking an event ticket.
  • Gender – provided voluntarily for event capacity management (male/female slots).
  • Anonymous Device ID – a randomly generated identifier stored locally on your device, used only to manage your bookings and prevent duplicate reservations. It is not linked to your real identity.
  • FCM Push Token – used to deliver push notifications. Linked only to your anonymous device ID.

We collect limited personal information (such as name and gender) only when voluntarily provided during booking. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, financial data, or device contacts from regular users.

This data collection is fully disclosed in our Google Play Data Safety section.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • User consent – when you voluntarily provide your name and gender during ticket booking.
  • Legitimate interest – to manage event capacity, prevent duplicate bookings, and protect the integrity of the service.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Manage and confirm event ticket reservations.
  • Enforce per-event capacity limits (male/female slots).
  • Send ticket confirmations and event reminders via push notification.
  • Send general event announcements.
  • Prevent abuse, duplicate bookings, and excessive requests.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Messaging) – Firebase acts as a data processor and processes data on our behalf for storage and push notifications. Firebase does not use your data for advertising purposes. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary – used by event creators to host event images. Cloudinary Privacy Policy

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above (Firebase, Cloudinary) who act as data processors strictly to operate the App. No data is shared with advertisers or any unrelated third parties.

7. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage via Google Firebase Firestore
  • Access controls and Firebase Security Rules
  • Rate limiting and abuse prevention mechanisms

8. Data Retention

We retain reservation data only as long as necessary for event operations and check-in management. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so:

  • Send a deletion request to official.bestevent@gmail.com
  • If available, use the in-app option to cancel or remove your reservation directly.

All deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

10. Push Notifications

The App may send push notifications for booking confirmations and event updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to book or view tickets.

11. Payments

All payments are made in cash at the event venue. The App does not collect, process, or store any financial or payment information.

12. Creator & Admin Accounts

Event creators and administrators log in using email and password credentials managed through Firebase Authentication. These accounts are created by the operator only and are not publicly self-registered. Creator account data is accessible only to authorized personnel.

13. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (processed within 7 days).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Disable push notifications via device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at official.bestevent@gmail.com.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
