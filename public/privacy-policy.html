Privacy Policy

Best Evento  |  Last updated: March 22, 2026

Best Evento ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Best Evento mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

Age Requirement: This App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Name – entered voluntarily when booking a ticket for an event.
  • Gender – selected voluntarily during ticket booking (used for event capacity management).
  • Anonymous Device ID – a randomly generated identifier stored locally on your device. It is never linked to your real identity and is used solely to manage your tickets and prevent duplicate bookings.
  • FCM Push Token – a Firebase Cloud Messaging token used to send you event notifications and ticket confirmations. It is linked only to your anonymous device ID.

We do not collect your email address, phone number, location, payment details, or any other personally identifiable information from regular users.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your event ticket reservation.
  • To enforce per-event capacity limits (male / female slots).
  • To send you ticket confirmation and event reminder notifications.
  • To send general announcements about new events via push notification.
  • To prevent abuse (e.g., duplicate bookings, excessive requests).

3. Firebase & Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Messaging) – stores reservation data and delivers push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudinary – used by event creators to host event images. Cloudinary Privacy Policy

No data is sold or shared with advertisers or any other third parties.

4. Creator & Admin Accounts

Event creators and administrators log in using email and password credentials managed through Firebase Authentication. These credentials are created and managed by the operator (Best Evento) and are not self-registered by the public. Creator account data is governed by Firebase's security practices and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

5. Data Storage & Security

Reservation data (name, gender, anonymous device ID, ticket ID) is stored in Google Firestore with access restricted by Firestore Security Rules. Your device ID is stored locally using SharedPreferences and is never transmitted beyond what is necessary for the service. We apply rate limiting to prevent abuse.

6. Data Retention

Reservation records are retained for operational purposes (event management, check-in scanning). You may request deletion of your reservation at any time by contacting us. Cancelled reservations are marked as cancelled and may be fully deleted upon request.

7. Push Notifications

The App requests permission to send push notifications. You may revoke this permission at any time through your device's system settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to book or view tickets.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about your device/reservations.
  • Request correction or deletion of your reservation data.
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Payments

All payments are made in cash at the venue. The App does not process, collect, or store any payment or financial information.

10. Cancellation Policy

Users may cancel their reservation at no charge. No refunds are issued after payment has been made at the venue.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
